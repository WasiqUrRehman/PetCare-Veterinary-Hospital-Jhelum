About PetCare Veterinary Hospital

At PetCare, we are dedicated to providing the highest standard of care for your cherished pets. Our experienced and compassionate team treats every animal with the kindness and respect they deserve. We believe in building a lasting relationship with you and your pet to ensure a lifetime of health and happiness.

Our state-of-the-art facility in Jhelum is equipped to handle everything from routine check-ups and vaccinations to advanced surgical procedures and emergency care. Trust us to be your partner in your pet's health journey.

Our Services

Routine Check-ups & Vaccinations

Preventative care is key to a long, healthy life. We provide thorough wellness exams and all necessary vaccinations.

Surgical Procedures

For unexpected injuries or illnesses, our clinic is prepared to provide urgent medical attention.
